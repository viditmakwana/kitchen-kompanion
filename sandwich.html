Sandwich Recipe

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ham


Step 1: Get bread and spread mayo .

Step 2: Put ham on top of mayo.

Step 3: Put tomato on top of ham.

Step 4: Put lettuce on top of tomato.

Step 5: Finish with another piece of bread.
